portfolio

I fell in love with creating art ever since the first time I picked up a paintbrush at age 4.

My aim is to convey a feeling of tranquility or melancholy by using harmonious colours and establishing a dream-like quality to my work. I love experimenting with visual texture and like to use colour intentionally to draw the eye in.

Mainly, I like to paint what brings me joy and peace.
